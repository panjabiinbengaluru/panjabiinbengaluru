Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

Who can join Panjabi in Bengaluru?

Anyone of Punjabi origin living in or around Bangalore is welcome to apply for membership. We're an inclusive community that celebrates Punjabi culture and connections.

How often do you organize events?

We organize community meetups monthly, career/business talks quarterly, and curated trips seasonally. Members also get exclusive invites to special events throughout the year.

How can brands or businesses collaborate with PIB?

We love meaningful brand partnerships! Reach out to us at business@panjabiinbengaluru.com to explore sponsorships, event collaborations, or brand associations.

How can I follow your events and updates?

Follow us on Instagram @panjabi.in.bengaluru for real-time updates, event announcements, and community moments.

I want to speak at one of your events. How do I get in touch?

We're always looking for inspiring speakers from the Punjabi community! Email us at admin@panjabiinbengaluru.com with a brief intro and your area of expertise.
