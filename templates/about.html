The Beginning

A Story Born
from Homesickness

It started simply — four Panjabis in Bangalore, missing the warmth of back home. Missing the banter, the food, the festivals, and the sense of belonging that comes so naturally in Panjab.

Mehakdeep, Karun, and Karanbir had each carved their own paths in Bangalore's fast-paced world. But they kept coming back to the same question: where are our people?

"We didn't want a Facebook group. We wanted a real community — something exclusive, something meaningful, something that actually brought people together."

— The Founding Team

So they built it. What started as small weekend meetups grew into a movement. Today, Panjabi in Bengaluru is the heartbeat of hundreds of Panjabis who now have a second home in the Garden City.

Our Mission

What Drives Everything
We Do

Three pillars form the foundation of everything Panjabi in Bengaluru stands for.

Belonging

Every Panjabi in Bangalore deserves a place that feels like home. We create that warmth — the laughter, the food, the familiarity that reminds you where you're from.

Growth

We believe in lifting each other up. Through career talks, business networking, and collaborative opportunities, we help every member reach their potential.

Culture

Keeping the spirit of Panjab alive — from Baisakhi celebrations to Diwali gatherings — we honour our roots while building our futures in Bengaluru.
