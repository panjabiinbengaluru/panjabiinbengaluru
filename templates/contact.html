FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Panjabi in Bengaluru.

Who can join Panjabi in Bengaluru?

Anyone with Panjabi roots living in Bangalore is welcome to apply. We celebrate shared culture, language, and heritage — whether you grew up in Panjab, Delhi, or anywhere else. We look for genuine community spirit and a desire to connect.

How often do you host events?

We host events regularly throughout the year — from intimate networking dinners and rooftop meetups to larger cultural celebrations and city trips. Members get priority access and advance notice for all events via our members-only WhatsApp group.

Is there a membership fee?

Membership details are shared after your application is reviewed and approved. We keep our community intentionally curated to maintain the quality of experience for all members. Reach out to us for current membership information.

Can brands or businesses partner with PIB?

Yes! We collaborate with select brands that align with our community's values. Email us at business@panjabiinbengaluru.com with your proposal and we'll get back to you within 48 hours.

How do I follow PIB on social media?

Follow us on Instagram for the latest events, highlights, and community moments. Search for @panjabiinbengaluru on Instagram and hit follow to stay updated on everything happening in the PIB universe.
